Course Description:

An introduction to the study and design of human/computer interfaces. The course will begin with an introduction to human factors - the design and support of systems for human use. Topics will include but are not limited to user modelling, task analysis, goal oriented design, command languages, grammars, menu and window systems, user documentation, and on-line help systems.
Katie Lee, Wenjie Lin, Mutaz Fattal, Kushal Baral - COSC 3P94 - Stage 2
